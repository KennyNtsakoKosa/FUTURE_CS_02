## Analysis Approach
Two phishing email samples were analyzed:
1. **Fake Bank Account Lock** – Impersonates a financial institution.
2. **Fake Netflix Suspension** – Impersonates a streaming service.

## Red Flags Identified
- Generic greetings ("Dear User", "Dear Valued Member")
- Suspicious sender domains (fake `.com`, `.top` domains)
- Urgency and fear-based language (24 hrs, 48 hrs)
- Fake links hiding real destinations
